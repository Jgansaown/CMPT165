Arduino 2x2x2 LED Cube Tutorial

Overview

A LED cube is a bunch of LEDs connected together in a cube arrangment. Since the LEDs are connected in a 3 dimensional pattern, it is able to display a 3D pattern, giving off a holographic view.

Materials and Equipments Needed

  • 8 LEDs
  • 4 resistors
  • Prototyping board
  • Wires
  • Soldering Iron

This LED cube will be powered by a Arduino Uno. However I will not be albe to go into the details of programming the Arduino here, as it would take too long so it will not be the focus here.
