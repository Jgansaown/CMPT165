Hero Powers in Hearthstone

Hearthstone: Heros of Warcraft is a turn-based online collectible card game. Players can pick from a total of nine classes, Druid, Hunter, Mage, Paladin, Priest, Rogue, Shaman, Warlock, and Warrior.

Each classes have different strengths and weaknesses, and their own unique Hero Power, which are abilities that can only be activated once a turn.

Hero Powers and class specific cards are the main differences between the nine classes and gives each classes their unique identity.
